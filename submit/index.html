§ 05 — Paper format

Submission requirements.

Template
IEEE conference proceedings template (US Letter, 2-column). LaTeX and Word versions are available from the IEEE author resources page ↗.
Length
4–6 pages including references and figures. Up to 2 additional pages may be purchased at camera-ready time.
Format
Anonymized PDF for review (no author names, affiliations, or self-identifying acknowledgements). Camera-ready version is non-anonymous.
Language
All papers must be written in English. Authors are encouraged to have papers proofread before submission.
Originality
Submissions must be original work not previously published or under simultaneous review elsewhere. Extended versions of conference papers are not eligible.
References
IEEE reference style. Include DOIs where available. Avoid excessive self-citation.
§ 06 — Review process

How papers are evaluated.

SISY uses a double-blind peer review process. Each paper receives a minimum of three independent reviews from the Program Committee.

Review criteria

  • Originality — novelty of the contribution
  • Technical quality — soundness of method and results
  • Clarity — quality of writing and presentation
  • Relevance — fit with the symposium's scope
  • Significance — potential impact on the field

Decisions

Possible outcomes are: accept, conditional accept (with required revisions before camera-ready), and reject. Reviewer comments are shared with authors at notification time.

The best papers of the symposium are eligible for Best Paper Awards announced during the closing session.

§ 07 — Ethics & policies

Publication policies.

Plagiarism
SISY adheres to the IEEE policy on plagiarism. All submissions are screened with similarity checking tools. Confirmed plagiarism results in rejection and notification of the authors' institution.
Generative AI
Use of generative AI tools must be explicitly disclosed in the acknowledgements or methods section. AI cannot be listed as an author.
Dual submission
Papers under review at another venue at the time of submission to SISY 2026 will be rejected without further consideration.
Conflicts of interest
Authors should declare relevant conflicts at submission time. Reviewers will be assigned with conflicts respected.
Copyright
Authors of accepted papers will sign the standard IEEE copyright form. Open-access publication options are available per IEEE policy.
No-show policy
Papers not presented in-person by a registered author will be marked "no-show" and removed from the IEEE Xplore submission.
