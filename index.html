§ 01 — About

A symposium on intelligent systems & informatics.

Now in its 24th year, SISY convenes researchers and practitioners working across computational intelligence, robotics, mechatronics, manufacturing, and applied informatics.

SISY 2026 brings together academics, engineers, and industry practitioners from across Europe and beyond to share new research, exchange ideas, and build collaborations between intelligent systems and engineering disciplines.

The symposium series began in 2003 and has been hosted by Óbuda University (Budapest) together with rotating partner universities across the region. The 2026 edition is co-hosted by Juraj Dobrila University of Pula at the Faculty of Economics and Tourism — a fitting setting for three days of discussion at the meeting point of theory and applied research.

§ 05 — Call for Papers

Submit your paper.

SISY 2026 is open for submissions. Papers meeting IEEE quality standards may be included in the IEEE Xplore Digital Library; the series is indexed by Scopus. At least one author must register and present in-person in Pula for inclusion in the proceedings.
