§ 03 — Keynotes

Two invited talks.

Each keynote opens its day with a 45-minute talk followed by Q&A. Topics span trustworthy human-machine systems and AI-powered computational models in healthcare.

Keynote · 01safety-by-design

Trustworthy Human-Machine Systems: Formal Methods for Safety-by-Design

How can we make human–machine systems verifiably safe by construction? This talk surveys formal methods for the design of trustworthy interactive systems — from automotive cooperation to industrial cobots — and presents recent results on safety-by-design across multi-agent settings.

BV
Bálint Varga
Karlsruhe Institute of Technology · DE
Keynote · 02digital twins

Artificial Intelligence-powered Computational Models for Theranostic Digital Twins

Theranostic digital twins combine imaging, simulation, and AI-driven computational models to personalize diagnosis and therapy. This keynote presents progress on AI-powered modeling for nuclear medicine and the open challenges of bringing these systems into routine clinical practice.

HZ
Habib Zaidi
Geneva University Hospital · CH
