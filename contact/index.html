Contact & committee.

SISY 2026 is co-organized by four universities and an IEEE technical committee, chaired by Levente Kovács of Óbuda University. All inquiries route through the Secretary General.

§ 01 — Contact

Get in touch.

For inquiries about submissions, special sessions, registration, payment, or general information, please contact the Secretary General directly.

Submissions & review

Paper submission is handled through the EasyChair portal. For questions about scope, special sessions, or the review process, write to the Secretary General.

Registration & payment

For registration form questions, invoice details, wire-transfer confirmation, or any issue with the payment process, please include your participant name and EasyChair paper ID.

Special sessions

Proposals for special sessions can be submitted through the same contact. Include a short description and a tentative list of contributors.
